# HR Analytics Dashboard

## 1. Project Title 
A dynamic and interactive HR Analytics dashboard built to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary trends, and job satisfaction.

---

## 2. Description
This Power BI dashboard helps HR teams explore employee data, identify attrition patterns, and uncover workforce insights.  
It enables data-driven decision-making to improve employee retention, engagement, and organizational planning.

---

## 3. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- Power BI Desktop – Data visualization & report creation  
- Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures and KPIs  
- Data Modeling – Relationships between HR tables  
- CSV Dataset – Source data  

---

## 4. Data Source
Dataset sourced from **Kaggle (HR Analytics dataset)** containing employee records such as:

- Age  
- Gender  
- Education  
- Salary  
- Job Role  
- Years at Company  
- Job Satisfaction  
- Attrition status  

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem
Employee attrition leads to increased hiring costs, productivity loss, and operational inefficiencies.  
HR teams often lack a clear, visual way to understand why employees leave and which factors influence attrition.

### • Goal of the Dashboard
To monitor workforce trends and identify key drivers of attrition using interactive visual analytics.

### • Walkthrough of Key Visuals
- KPI Cards → Total employees, attrition count, attrition rate, average age, salary, tenure  
- Attrition by Age Group → Identify high-risk age segments  
- Attrition by Salary Slab → Salary-based trends  
- Attrition by Job Role → Role-wise employee turnover  
- Attrition by Education & Gender → Demographic analysis  
- Job Satisfaction Matrix → Satisfaction vs attrition insights  
- Years at Company → Tenure-based attrition trends  

### • Business Impact & Insights
- Identifies departments and roles with high attrition  
- Highlights low salary slabs linked to higher turnover  
- Helps HR design better retention and engagement strategies  
- Supports strategic workforce planning
